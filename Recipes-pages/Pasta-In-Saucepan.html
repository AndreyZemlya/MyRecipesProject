Text tutorial

  1. Boil the pasta according to the instructions on the package.
  2. Finely chop the onion.
  3. Crush the garlic or chop it finely.
  4. Place a skillet over medium heat, pour in vegetable oil. Add the onion and garlic, stir with a spatula.
  5. Roast the vegetables for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add the mince and fry for 10-12 minutes, stirring with a spatula.
  7. Add tomato paste, stir, warm for 2 minutes.
  8. Pour in the hot water, heat the contents for 5 minutes.
  9. Season with salt and pepper. Add the pasta, stir, heat for another 5 minutes.
  10. Serve the Flotsky-style pasta hot. If desired, you can sprinkle macaroni with grated cheese and herbs.
