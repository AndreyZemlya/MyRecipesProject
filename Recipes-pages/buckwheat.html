Text tutorial

A list of what you'll need to cook:

  • Buckwheat
  • A saucepan
  • Some seasonings

Cooking process

  1. Pour all the buckwheat into the saucepan
  2. Rinse the buckwheat 3 times in water.
  3. Fill the buckwheat with water so that there is 1.5 times more water than buckwheat.
  4. Put the saucepan on maximum heat and add the seasonings you want.
  5. Stir every 3 minutes and cook until boiling.
  6. Once boiling, simmer for another 5 minutes and turn off the heat
  7. Cover the saucepan with a lid and let the buckwheat stand for about 10-20 minutes.
  8. It's done! You can put it on plates and add some sauce and enjoy the buckwheat.
