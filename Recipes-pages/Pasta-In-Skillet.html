Text tutorial

  1. Put a skillet on maximum heat and pour some sunflower oil in it.
  2. When the skillet is hot add the pasta and fry it for a couple minutes.
  3. Pour boiling water into the skillet and stir in the pasta.
  4. Add a little salt and desired seasonings and cover the pan with a lid, stirring every 3 minutes, until almost all the water has evaporated.
  5. When there is almost no water left you can turn off the heat and enjoy the pasta.
